What's a Bug?

Before you can fix something, you need to understand what went wrong. Byte lives for this part.

📖
Read · 2 min

"Hi! I'm Byte. I'm a glitch cat — which means I LOVE finding mistakes. Most cats chase mice. I chase bugs."

A bug is just a mistake in code. It could be a typo. It could be instructions in the wrong order. It could be a missing piece. Bugs are super normal — even professional coders have them every single day.

The cool thing? Every bug is actually a puzzle. And puzzles have solutions. That means when you find a bug, you're not failing — you're one step closer to figuring it out.

Byte's rule: Don't panic. Look closer. The answer is always in the code somewhere.
