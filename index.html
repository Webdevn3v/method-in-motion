Curriculum Tiers

Progressive learning
from 4 to 12.

Every child starts as a Spark and grows into a Coder — building real skills at every stage.

Tier 01  ·  Sparks
Sparks
Ages 4–6
Patterns, sequences, and creative logic — no screen required. Tracing, sorting, matching, and hands-on discovery to build a coder's mindset early.
ZEN BYTE
Tier 02  ·  Builders
Builders
Ages 7–9
Functions, loops, cause and effect — building real things step by step. Drag-and-drop activities, simple conditionals, and website fundamentals.
NOVA LOOP
Tier 03  ·  Coders
Coders
Ages 10–12
Debugging, events, sound and interaction — real intro to HTML/CSS concepts and event-driven thinking. Break things on purpose and fix them better.
