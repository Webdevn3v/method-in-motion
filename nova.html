Parts of a Page

Nova never builds without a plan. Before you write a line of code, you need to know what you're building and what pieces you need.

📖
Read · 2 min

"Hi! I'm Nova. I build things. Robots, websites, apps — if it has parts that fit together, I'm interested. And websites? They always have the same parts."

Every website you've ever visited has the same basic structure — like how every house has walls, a roof, and a door. The pieces might look different, but they're always there.

The four main parts are: a Navigation (how you get around), a Hero (the big first thing you see), Content (the actual stuff — text, images, cards), and a Footer (the bottom with links and info).

Nova's rule: understand the parts before you build. You wouldn't build a robot without knowing what every piece does.
