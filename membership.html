Questions

Is the free tier actually free?
Yes — no credit card, no trial period, no catch. Create an account and Lesson 1 for all 6 characters is yours forever.
What do the paid tiers unlock?
Lessons 2–4 for every character, plus project challenges and bonus content depending on your tier. The free tier only includes Lesson 1 per character.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — cancel anytime from your account, no questions asked. Your access stays active through the end of your billing period. Your free account stays forever.
Which paid tier is right for my kid?
Use the age ranges as a guide — Sparks for 4–6, Builders for 7–9, Coders for 10–12. Every kid is different though, so pick what feels right. You can always switch.
What about the workbooks?
Workbooks are separate one-time purchases at $7.99 each. A membership gives you the on-site lessons — workbooks are printable extras you keep forever.
